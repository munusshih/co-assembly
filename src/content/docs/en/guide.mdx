## Glossary Links

[Specialized terms](/en/meta/glossary/) in the bylaws are highlighted as links. Click to view their definitions.

Examples:

- [Worker-owner](/en/meta/glossary/#worker-owner)
- [Associate worker-owner](/en/meta/glossary/#associate-worker-owner)
- [Common Pool](/en/meta/glossary/#pool)
- [Consensus decision-making](/en/meta/glossary/#consensus)

## Cross-references

The bylaws use a custom ID system to link between related articles:

- Internal link format: `[Article name](/en/bylaws/chapter/#article-id)`
- Example: [MEM-01](/en/bylaws/02-membership/#mem-01) links to Article MEM-01 in Chapter 2

## Aside Components

The bylaws use Starlight's built-in Aside components for supplementary notes:

:::note
A general supplementary note.
:::

:::tip[Practical Tip]
A useful suggestion or best practice.
:::

:::caution
An important item requiring attention.
:::

:::danger[Critical Warning]
A serious warning or legal notice.
:::

## Navigation

- **Sidebar**: Full chapter structure on the left
- **Table of Contents**: Current page headings on the right
- **Previous/Next**: Read sequentially at the bottom of each page
- **Edit this page**: Edit directly on GitHub from the bottom of any page
